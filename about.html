About CnB Cookies

We are a sucker for everything chocolate, especially when it comes wrapped in the form of soft, fresh, gooey cookies. As a passionate foodie—like you—we spent a lot of time searching for the kind of cookies that would melt in our mouths at first bite. It was hard to find a place that served the most heavenly and affordable cookies without using eggs, maida, or cheap chocolate varieties.

So, we created one!

CnB Cookies is the result of our immense love for choco-stuffed cookies, and our determination to eat healthy products baked with top-quality ingredients. Everything we bake comes straight out of our oven. With us, enjoy the softest, gooeiest cookies with no preservatives that are vegetarian and healthy!
